Part 1

Canva Webpage Structure — Section by Section

Use a vertical webpage format in Canva (recommended size: 1080 × 6000 px or longer). Palette: Navy #0D1B3E · Gold #C9A84C · Light Blue #4A9FD4 · Soft Green #5BAD8A · White · Off-white #F4F8FC.

SECTION 1HERO

Background: Dark navy gradient. Center: Logo in a gold circle border. Below: Company name in large bold white text. Slogan in small uppercase light blue text. Gold pill badge: "🎾 Current Celebrity: Novak Djokovic"

Icons/Deco: Add tennis ball graphics (green, blue) as transparent overlays in corners.

Layout: Full-width, centered, tall enough to feel like a real hero banner (~700–800 px tall).

SECTION 2CONTACT BAR

Background: White with a gold bottom border. Layout: 5 items in a horizontal row, separated by thin lines. Each item: small navy circle icon + text. Icons: 📍✉️📞📷👍

SECTION 3PREV. CLIENTS

Background: White. Title: "Previous Clients" with gold underline bar. Cards: 5 rounded white cards in a 2–3 column grid. Each card: left gold border + navy icon box + name + description.

SECTION 4OUR TEAM

Background: Off-white/gray. Title: "Our Team". Cards: 5 white cards with rounded top blue border. Each: circular photo (or initials), name, role badge (navy+gold), short description, email.

Layout: 2 columns, last card centered OR 2+3 grid.

SECTION 5DJOKOVIC BIO

Background: White. Layout: Split card — left: Djokovic photo on dark navy background. Right: Bio text with name in large bold navy font, 3 paragraphs, stat pills at bottom (24 Slams, #1 Ranking, 20+ Years, 4 Slam Types).

SECTION 6STATS GRAPH

Background: Light gray. Title: "Statistics Graph" + Week 13 label. Chart: Line + bar combo chart (Grand Slam titles 2008–2026). Export from HTML file and paste as image in Canva.

Below chart: 6 small analysis cards in 2–3 column grid. Each: bolded year + key vocabulary (plateau, sharp rise, slight dip, upward trend, level off, steady growth).

SECTION 7GUIDELINES

Background: Dark navy. Title: White "Guidelines to Liaise with Novak Djokovic". List: 5 numbered items. Each: gold numbered circle + white card with text. Light blue paragraph intro text above the list.

SECTION 8VISION · MISSION · VALUES

Background: White. Title: "Vision · Mission · Values". 3 cards side by side: Vision (navy bg, gold text), Mission (blue bg, white text), Values (green bg, white text). Each has an icon on top.

SECTION 9EMAIL (WK 5)

Background: Light gray. Style: A "browser window" design — navy top bar with 3 colored dots + email label. White email body below with From/To/Date/Subject header, then email text. Add gold border-bottom to subject line.

SECTION 10LETTER (WK 9)

Background: White. Style: Formal letter card — thick gold top border, sender info top left, date, "Dear Novak Djokovic," greeting, 4 letter paragraphs, closing signature. Add large faint "GCR" watermark text in background.

SECTION 11TRAINING (WK 10)

Background: Light gray. Title: "Training Program & Schedule". 3 cards: Marketing (blue icon), Tech Skills (gold icon), Customer Service (green icon). Below: a 4-column schedule table with week/area/activity/duration. Color-coded badges for each area.

SECTION 12REPORT (WK 15)

Background: White. Style: Report card — dark navy header with title + author line. White body with 5 numbered points, each: gold numbered circle + heading + paragraph. Clean, professional look.

FOOTER

Background: Dark navy. Content: Logo text, slogan, navigation links, divider line, contact info, copyright line. All text in white/gold/light blue.

Oral Presentation Script (5–8 Minutes)

Divided among five team members. Speak naturally, clearly, and at a steady pace. Estimated total time: 6–7 minutes.

Kevin — General Manager

"Good morning, everyone. My name is Kevin Espinoza, and I am the General Manager of Global Champion Reps.

Today, we are very happy to present our final sports representation agency website. Our company is called Global Champion Reps, and our slogan is Next Level Players.

We are a professional sports agency based in London, England. Our main goal is to represent elite athletes, connect them with international opportunities, sponsorships, and media partnerships.

Our current celebrity is Novak Djokovic — one of the greatest tennis players in history. Throughout this presentation, each member of our team will explain the different sections of our website.

Let me now pass the word to Diego, our Marketing Manager."

Diego — Marketing Manager

"Thank you, Kevin. My name is Diego Lizarraga, and I am the Marketing Manager.

On our website, you can find the contact information for our company. You can reach us by email at contact@globalchampionreps.com, by phone at +44 20 7946 0857, or on Instagram and Facebook.

In the Previous Clients section, you can see that we have already worked with important athletes and brands. Our previous clients include Alexander Zverev, Adidas, the London Elite Sports Summit, the NextGen Tennis Development Program, and the Elite Performance Sports Camp.

We are very proud of these collaborations because they show that we are a professional and reliable agency. Our marketing strategy focuses on increasing our visibility through social media and building strong brand partnerships.

Now, I will pass the word to Esmeralda, our Public Relations Manager."

Esmeralda — Public Relations Manager

"Thank you, Diego. My name is Esmeralda Valentina Olvera Sánchez, and I am the Public Relations Manager.

In the Celebrity section of our website, you can read about our current athlete — Novak Djokovic. Djokovic is a professional tennis player from Serbia. He was born on May 22, 1987, in Belgrade. He has won 24 Grand Slam titles, including the Australian Open, Wimbledon, the French Open, and the US Open.

He is known for his exceptional physical endurance, flexibility, and mental strength. These qualities have made him one of the most dominant athletes in modern tennis.

In the Guidelines section, we explain how to communicate with the celebrity. All requests must be submitted at least two weeks in advance. All media appearances must follow contractual agreements, and all personal contact must go through our agency. This protects both the athlete and our partners.

Thank you. Now I'll hand it over to Johanna."

Johanna — Social Media Manager

"Thank you, Esmeralda. My name is Johanna Ramirez Hernandez, and I am the Social Media Manager.

In the Our Team section, you can find a profile for each member of our staff. We have five team members: Kevin, the General Manager; Diego, the Marketing Manager; Esmeralda, the Public Relations Manager; myself as Social Media Manager; and José Ricardo, the Finance Manager. Each profile includes a photo, our role, and our contact email.

I was also responsible for the Website Improvement Report. In that report, we identified problems with the old website — it was outdated, and important information was hard to find. The new website is modern, clean, and fully responsive for mobile devices. It includes all the sections required, and the design is attractive and easy to navigate.

I am very happy with how the final webpage looks. It truly represents our company in a professional way.

Now, José Ricardo will present the graph, the training program, and the conclusion."

José Ricardo — Finance Manager

"Thank you, Johanna. My name is José Ricardo Vera Juárez, and I am the Finance Manager.

Let me start with the Statistics Graph. This line graph illustrates the upward trend of Novak Djokovic's Grand Slam titles from 2008 to 2026.

At the beginning, from 2008 to 2010, the number of titles remained steady. There was a plateau in his Grand Slam count. However, in 2011 and again in 2015, there was a sharp rise in trophies. His success clearly peaked during these years. In 2017, a slight dip in performance occurred due to injuries. But from 2018 to 2023, the graph shows a strong upward trend with steady growth. In the final years, from 2024 to 2026, the figures level off at 24 titles. Overall, the graph shows a dramatic upward trajectory over nearly two decades.

Now, about our Training Program. Our staff training plan focuses on three key areas: Marketing, Technological Skills, and Customer Service. Each area has a two-week training schedule. By improving these areas, our team will become more professional, efficient, and competitive.

To conclude, our agency — Global Champion Reps — has worked hard to create a complete and professional website that represents everything we have learned in this course. We are proud to present this final project and we hope you enjoy it.

Thank you very much. We are happy to answer any questions you may have."
