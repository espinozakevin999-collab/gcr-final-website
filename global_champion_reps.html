Professional Tennis Player NOVAK
DJOKOVIC

Novak Djokovic is a professional tennis player from Serbia and is widely considered one of the greatest players in the history of tennis. He was born on May 22, 1987, in Belgrade, Serbia.

Throughout his career, Djokovic has won numerous Grand Slam titles, including the Australian Open, Wimbledon, the French Open, and the US Open. He has also held the world number one ranking multiple times and has broken several records in professional tennis.

He is known for his exceptional physical endurance, flexibility, and mental strength, which allow him to perform at the highest level. His dedication and consistency have made him one of the most dominant athletes in modern tennis.

Website Improvement Report

Improving the Global Champion Reps Website

Prepared by: Johanna Ramírez Hernández · Global Champion Reps

1

Introduction

This report outlines ways to improve the website of Global Champion Reps, whose slogan is "Next Level Players." As a company based in London, England, specializing in the representation of high-performance athletes, our website should communicate professionalism and excellence.

2

Problems with the Old Website

The previous website did not clearly present our services or athlete profiles. The design appeared outdated and some important information was difficult to locate. As a result, potential clients and sponsors may have left the website without understanding the full range of services we offer.

3

Layout of the New Website

The new website features a modern and attractive design with clear navigation menus. The homepage highlights our slogan, athlete achievements, and company mission. Furthermore, the website is fully responsive to ensure easy access from mobile devices.

4

Information on the New Website

The website includes detailed athlete profiles, sponsorship opportunities, success stories, company services, and updated news. Visible contact information and details about our London headquarters are also provided.

5

Recommendations & Conclusion

A more user-friendly and informative website strengthens the company's image. It is recommended that Global Champion Reps continue to update its website regularly to improve communication, attract new athletes and sponsors, and support future business growth.
