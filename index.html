Who We Are

About the Company

Global Champion Reps is a sports representation agency based in London, England. We help high-performance athletes build their careers, secure partnerships, and grow their personal brands at an international level.

Vision

To become a leading agency in the representation of emerging sports talents — helping athletes reach their full potential at national and international levels.

Mission

To support athletes with professional guidance, new opportunities, and a dedicated team that accompanies them throughout their entire sports career.

Ethics & Values

  • Professionalism — Excellence in every project.
  • Respect — We value athletes, partners, and clients equally.
  • Transparency — Clear and honest communication at all times.
  • Integrity — Ethical and responsible decisions.
  • Commitment — Supporting the growth of every athlete we represent.

Current Athlete

Novak Djokovic

Professional Tennis Player · Serbia NOVAK
DJOKOVIC

Novak Djokovic is a professional tennis player from Serbia, born on May 22, 1987, in Belgrade. He is widely considered one of the greatest players in the history of the sport.

He has won 24 Grand Slam titles, including the Australian Open, Wimbledon, the French Open, and the US Open, and has held the World No. 1 ranking multiple times.

Djokovic is known for his physical endurance, mental strength, and remarkable consistency — qualities that make him one of the most dominant athletes in modern sport.
